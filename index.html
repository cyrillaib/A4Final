Abandoned Mines & Tailings in Québec

GEOG 464 – Final Project (A4 Demo)

Project focus

This demo explores abandoned mines and tailings sites in Québec, with a focus on proximity to population centres, Indigenous communities, and surface water (lakes).

Impact filter

Clicked site details

Click on a mine / tailings site on the map to see its impact class, a simple buffer, the nearest town, nearest Indigenous / northern community, and nearest major water body (manual list).

About the buffers

The red circle around a site is not a real risk model. It is a simple, hypothetical buffer to help visualise potential areas of influence for discussion in class.
